Name: Michael Jackson

Biography: Michael Jackson, born on August 29, 1958, in Gary, Indiana, was an American singer, songwriter, and dancer known as the "King of Pop." He started his music career at a young age with his brothers in the Jackson 5, quickly becoming the group's lead singer. His solo career took off in the 1970s, with groundbreaking albums like Off the Wall (1979), Thriller (1982), and Bad (1987). Thriller remains the best-selling album of all time.

Most representative album: Thriller

Name: Bruno Mars

Biography: Bruno Mars, born Peter Gene Hernandez on October 8, 1985, in Honolulu, Hawaii, is an American singer, songwriter, and producer. He grew up in a musical family and began performing at a young age. After moving to Los Angeles, he gained recognition for his songwriting skills before breaking out as a solo artist with his debut album Doo-Wops & Hooligans (2010). Known for his eclectic style blending pop, funk, R&B, and reggae, Mars has produced hits like "Just the Way You Are," "Locked Out of Heaven," and "Uptown Funk." He has won multiple Grammy Awards and is celebrated for his energetic performances and smooth vocals.

Most representative album: 24K Magic

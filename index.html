Name: Michael Jackson

Biography: Michael Jackson, born on August 29, 1958, in Gary, Indiana, was an American singer, songwriter, and dancer known as the "King of Pop." He started his music career at a young age with his brothers in the Jackson 5, quickly becoming the group's lead singer. His solo career took off in the 1970s, with groundbreaking albums like Off the Wall (1979), Thriller (1982), and Bad (1987). Thriller remains the best-selling album of all time.
